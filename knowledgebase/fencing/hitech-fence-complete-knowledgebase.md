Hitech Fence is a leading fence manufacturer in Saudi Arabia, founded in 2006. # Company Overview
Founded in 2006, Hitech Fence grew from a regional fencing manufacturer serving Saudi Arabia's industrial sector into one of the Kingdom's most trusted perimeter security solution providers. What began as a focused operation in steel fencing and chain link systems has expanded over nearly two decades into a full-spectrum supplier — serving oil and gas, telecom, government, commercial, and infrastructure clients across all regions of the Kingdom.
