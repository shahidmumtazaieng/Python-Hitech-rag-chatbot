# Summary (Primary retrieval anchor)
HCIS Category 1 represents the **highest security level** for perimeter fencing under the Saudi High Commission for Industrial Security (HCIS) SEC-02 directive. It is mandatory for FSC Class 1 critical facilities (e.g., major oil & gas installations, refineries, strategic power plants). The system uses a **multi-layered defense** approach including an anti-vehicle barrier, a 3m high anti-personnel delay-rated fence with triple concertina razor wire, extensive clear zones, patrol roads, lighting, sensors, and surveillance. Hitech Fence & Steel supplies robust chain link and welded mesh systems engineered to support HCIS Category 1 compliant projects with Saudi-made durability and quality.

# HCIS Category 1 Overview
Category 1 fencing is required where the highest level of protection against both vehicle-borne improvised explosive devices (VBIED) and pedestrian intrusion is essential. It combines physical barriers, delay features, detection, and response facilitation into a holistic perimeter system.

The Category 1 system consists of three main layout areas:
- External Interface Area
- Anti-Personnel Fence Area
- Internal Interface Area

# Detailed Components of HCIS Category 1 Fence System

## 1. External Interface Area
- **Outer Patrol Road**: Minimum 4.6m wide (can use existing roads under specific conditions).
- **External Clear Zone**: 6m wide (measured from the inside edge of the patrol road to the anti-personnel fence).
- Purpose: Provides space for patrol vehicles, early warning, and unobstructed visibility. Must remain free of vegetation and obstructions (maximum vegetation height 152mm).

## 2. Anti-Personnel Fence Area
- **Fence Height**: Minimum 3 meters.
- **Fence Type**: Rigid welded mesh or high-security chain link (anti-climb / anti-cut design with small mesh openings).
- **Topping**: Three rolls of concertina / razor wire:
  - One roll mounted on top of the fence.
  - Second roll mounted on the front top.
  - Third roll mounted on the external bottom of the fence.
- **Delay Rating**: Must be tested and certified for forced entry resistance (commonly referenced to ASTM F2781-15). Provides significant delay (e.g., several minutes) against aggressive cutting, climbing, and breaching tools.
- **Materials**: High-tensile steel, hot-dip galvanized with optional PVC coating for corrosion resistance in Saudi desert conditions.
- **Anti-Vehicle Layer**: Includes a certified crash-rated anti-vehicle barrier (AVB) compliant with SEC-06, often ASTM F2656 M50 or equivalent rating.

## 3. Internal Interface Area
- **Inner Patrol Road**: Minimum 4.6m wide.
- **Internal Clear Zone**: 10m wide (measured from the inside edge of the inner patrol road to the anti-personnel fence).
- **Additional Components** (installed within the internal clear zone):
  - Intrusion detection sensors (per SEC-05).
  - Surveillance devices (CCTV, thermal cameras, etc.).
  - Security lighting fully compliant with SEC-04.
- Purpose: Ensures clear visibility, rapid response by security forces, and effective operation of detection systems.

# Key Performance Requirements for Category 1
- **Delay Time**: Designed to deter, detect, and significantly delay intruders, giving security teams critical response time.
- **Clear Zones**: Maintained free of obstructions for full visibility and sensor effectiveness.
- **Integration**: The fence must integrate seamlessly with gates (SEC-03), lighting, sensors, and overall facility security systems.
- **Installation**: Professional installation with proper foundations, secure welding, and full documentation for HCIS verification.
- **Environmental Adaptation**: Materials and coatings selected for extreme heat, sand, and corrosion in the Kingdom.
