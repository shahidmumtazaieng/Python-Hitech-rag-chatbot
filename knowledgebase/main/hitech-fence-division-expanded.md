# Division Overview
Hitech Fence & Steel is your trusted partner in Saudi Arabia for securing your world. Established in 2010 as Hitech Fence and Steel Industries, the division has grown into a complete steel solution provider. We combine unmatched security, durability, and innovation with Saudi excellence, delivering top-quality products, exceptional service, and outstanding value.

We specialize in:
- High-quality reinforced steel bars (Grade 60) for construction
- Comprehensive fencing systems
- Custom steel fabrication

All products are proudly Saudi-made, designed for the Kingdom’s harsh climate and critical infrastructure requirements.
