# Summary (Use this as primary retrieval anchor)
Hitech Steel Industries is a proud Saudi-owned industrial group and the parent company of multiple specialized divisions. Founded on quality, innovation, customer focus, and integrity, we deliver reliable fencing, security, storage, landscaping, and steel solutions across the Kingdom in line with Vision 2030.

# Company Overview
Hitech Steel Industries is the parent company to various industrial divisions including Hitech Fence, Hitech Gabion, Hitech Barrier, Hitech Automation, Racks and Partition. We boast a sterling reputation in Saudi Arabia as the go-to partner for reliable fencing, security, storage, and landscaping solutions. We are a Saudi-based manufacturer committed to Saudi excellence and Vision 2030.

# Our History & Timeline
- **2008**: Established Nartel for telecom, power, construction services and trading fencing products from UAE.  
- **2010**: Established Hitech Fence and Steel industries for fencing products.  
- **2017**: Both Nartel and Hitech Fence acquired by Hitech UK. Business now under Hitech UK ownership.  
- **2021**: Bought running retail shop for hardware and established Hitech Trading investment license.  

Hitech has been innovating Saudi solutions that transform lives worldwide since 2008.
