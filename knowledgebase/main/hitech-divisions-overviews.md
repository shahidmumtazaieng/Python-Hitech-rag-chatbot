# Summary (Primary retrieval anchor)
Hitech Steel Industries is the parent company of a diversified group of specialized industrial divisions in Saudi Arabia. We deliver high-quality, Saudi-made solutions in fencing, security, erosion control, UPVC, barriers, galvanizing, storage, and smart safety products. All divisions align with Saudi Vision 2030 through local manufacturing, innovation, sustainability, and excellence.

# Parent Company Overview
Hitech Steel Industries serves as the parent entity for multiple specialized divisions. We have built a strong reputation in Saudi Arabia as a reliable partner for fencing, security, storage, landscaping, and industrial solutions. Our focus remains on quality, innovation, customer-centricity, and integrity while supporting Vision 2030 through British-standard quality, advanced manufacturing, and local talent development.

We offer a wide portfolio including steel structures, UPVC windows and doors, fencing systems, spin galvanizing, gabions, barriers, racks & partitions, and smart solutions. Headquartered in Riyadh’s 2nd Industrial Area, we serve clients such as Saudi Aramco, government projects, contractors, and commercial entities.

# Detailed Division Overviews

## 1. Hitech Fence & Steel
**Overview**: Leading manufacturer of reinforced steel bars and high-quality fencing solutions in Saudi Arabia. We provide complete steel solutions with unmatched security, durability, and innovation.  
**Focus Areas**: Perimeter security, industrial fencing, decorative barriers, HCIS & Aramco compliant systems.  
**Key Highlights**: Saudi-made products designed for airports, railways, oil & gas, refineries, and critical infrastructure. We combine robust protection with aesthetic appeal.  
**Target Sectors**: Industrial facilities, construction, government, oil & gas.  
**Tagline from Site**: "Securing your property with high-quality fencing solutions."

## 2. Hitech Gabions
**Overview**: Saudi Arabia’s leading provider of gabion solutions, specializing in durable erosion control and landscaping products built to last.  
**Focus Areas**: Gabion boxes, mattresses, retaining walls, and architectural features for environmental protection and landscaping.  
**Key Highlights**: Engineered for longevity in harsh Saudi conditions; used in infrastructure, government, and commercial projects.  
**Target Sectors**: Erosion control, landscaping, civil engineering, municipal projects.  
**Tagline from Site**: "Durable erosion control & landscaping solutions built to last."

## 3. Maskni Alzaki (UPVC Division)
**Overview**: Provides premium, stylish UPVC doors and windows along with eco-friendly WPC doors in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.  
**Focus Areas**: Energy-efficient, durable, and modern home & office transformation solutions.  
**Key Highlights**: Designed to enhance aesthetics, security, and comfort while withstanding the Saudi climate.  
**Target Sectors**: Residential villas, commercial buildings, real estate developers.  
**Tagline from Site**: "Transform your home with stylish UPVC doors & windows in KSA."

## 4. Hitech Barrier
**Overview**: Offers advanced security and access control solutions including automatic boom barriers, pedestrian barriers, bollards, parking locks, and advertising barriers.  
**Focus Areas**: Vehicle and pedestrian traffic control, high-security perimeter protection.  
**Key Highlights**: Combines technology, safety, and reliability to stop intruders and manage access effectively. Some solutions integrate marketing opportunities.  
**Target Sectors**: Airports, ports, industrial sites, government facilities, parking areas.  
**Tagline from Site**: "Advanced barrier solutions to stop intruders and control access."

## 5. Spin Galvanized (Hitech Basics)
**Overview**: Specializes in protecting small steel parts using advanced spin galvanizing technology.  
**Focus Areas**: Fasteners, brackets, clamps, cable accessories, pipe supports, and other small components.  
**Key Highlights**: Provides uniform, thread-safe galvanization with quick turnaround; compliant with international standards.  
**Target Sectors**: Power & utilities, solar, infrastructure, fabrication workshops.  
**Tagline from Site**: "Protecting small steel parts with spin galvanizing technology."

## 6. Hitech Racks & Partition
**Overview**: Delivers unmatched quality Saudi-made racking and partition systems for efficient space utilization.  
**Focus Areas**: Heavy-duty warehouse racking, commercial shelving, office & industrial partitions, custom storage solutions.  
**Key Highlights**: Designed for safety, adjustability, and maximum space optimization in industrial and commercial environments.  
**Target Sectors**: Warehouses, factories, retail, offices, logistics.  
**Tagline from Site**: "Unmatched quality with Saudi-made racks and partitions."

## 7. Hitech eSmart
**Overview**: Innovates bathroom safety and hygiene solutions with advanced smart technology.  
**Focus Areas**: Smart toilet safety rails and related bathroom accessibility products.  
**Key Highlights**: Focuses on user safety, convenience, and integration into modern and public facilities.  
**Target Sectors**: Hospitals, public buildings, smart cities, residential care.  
**Tagline from Site**: "Innovating bathroom safety with advanced smart toilet rails."
