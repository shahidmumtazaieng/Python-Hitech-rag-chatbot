# Key Fencing Standards & Compliance
Hitech Fence solutions are engineered to meet stringent Saudi security and industrial requirements:

### 1. HCIS Compliance (High Commission for Industrial Security)
- Mandatory for strategic industries, energy sector, and high-security facilities in Saudi Arabia.
- Covers physical security, perimeter fencing, access control, and integration with surveillance systems.
- Requirements typically include:
  - Minimum fence height and strength
  - Anti-climb and anti-cut features
  - Resistance to weather, forced entry, and bolt cutters
  - Proper installation procedures and testing
- Hitech Fence is recognized as a trusted provider of HCIS-compliant fencing solutions.

### 2. Aramco Approval & Compliance
- Products meet Saudi Aramco’s rigorous vendor and technical specifications for oil & gas, refineries, and related infrastructure.
- Emphasis on heavy-duty steel construction, durable protective coatings, and long-term performance in harsh desert environments.
- Aramco-approved fencing ensures reliability for critical energy sector projects.

### 3. Material & Manufacturing Standards
- **Reinforced Steel Bars**: Grade 60 (common in construction and structural support for fencing).
- **Galvanization & Coatings**: In-house coating processes for corrosion protection. Products designed for Saudi climate durability.
- **General Manufacturing**: Adheres to international best practices for welding, mesh formation, and assembly to ensure consistent strength and finish.

### 4. Additional Quality & Testing References
- Products undergo rigorous testing for strength, durability, and performance.
- Compliance with relevant ASTM standards is referenced in group galvanizing processes (e.g., ASTM A123/A153 for zinc coatings), supporting overall corrosion resistance in fencing components.
- Focus on thread-safe, uniform coatings where applicable, and smooth surface finish.
