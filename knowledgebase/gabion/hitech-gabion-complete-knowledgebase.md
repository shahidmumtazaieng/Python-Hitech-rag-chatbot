- **ASTM A-90** - Weight of Coating on Iron and Steel Articles

# Common Customer Questions & Answers (FAQ)

## Products & Specifications

**Q: What sizes of gabion boxes do you offer?**
**A:** Standard sizes are 2x1x1m, 3x1x1m, 4x1x1m, and 5x1x1m. Custom sizes are available for specific project requirements.

**Q: What wire diameter do you use for gabions?**
**A:** We use wire diameters ranging from 2.7mm to 4.0mm depending on the application. The selection depends on structural requirements and load conditions.

**Q: Are your gabions galvanized?**
**A:** Yes, all our gabions are hot-dip galvanized with a minimum coating of 275 g/m². We also offer PVC coated and Galfan coated options for enhanced corrosion resistance.

**Q: What is the mesh opening size?**
**A:** Standard mesh openings are 80x100mm and 100x120mm using double-twist hexagonal mesh construction. Custom mesh sizes are available.

**Q: What standards do your gabions meet?**
**A:** Our products meet ASTM A974, ASTM A975, ASTM A641, ASTM A-90, and ASTM A-185 international standards.

**Q: Can you provide PVC coated gabions in colors?**
**A:** Yes, we offer PVC coated gabions in various colors including green, black, and grey for aesthetic applications.

## Applications & Usage

**Q: What are gabions used for?**
**A:** Gabions are used for retaining walls, erosion control, channel lining, MSE walls, landscaping, fencing, noise barriers, and architectural decoration.

**Q: Are gabions suitable for retaining walls?**
**A:** Yes, gabion retaining walls are highly effective for heights up to 10m. They are flexible, permeable, and cost-effective compared to concrete walls.

**Q: Can gabions be used for erosion control?**
**A:** Absolutely. Gabion mattresses (Reno mattresses) are specifically designed for riverbank protection, channel lining, and erosion control.

**Q: Do gabions allow vegetation growth?**
**A:** Yes, the permeable design allows vegetation to grow through the gabions, creating a natural appearance over time.

**Q: What should I fill gabions with?**
**A:** Gabions should be filled with hard, durable stone (granite, basalt, limestone). Stone size should be 100-200mm, larger than the mesh opening.
