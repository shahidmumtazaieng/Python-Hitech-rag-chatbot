# Common Customer Questions & Answers (FAQ)

## Products & Specifications

**Q: What barrier types do you offer for parking?**
**A:** We offer boom barriers, parking management systems, ticket dispensers, card readers, and license plate recognition systems for complete parking access control.

**Q: Are your barriers automated?**
**A:** Yes, all our barriers can be fully automated with remote control, access cards, mobile apps, or integration with access control systems.

**Q: What brands do you distribute?**
**A:** We are the exclusive distributor in Saudi Arabia for FAAC (Italy), Parklio (smart barriers), Instaboom, and Sosec (solar barriers).

**Q: Can barriers integrate with access control?**
**A:** Absolutely. Our barriers integrate with RFID, card readers, intercoms, biometric systems, license plate recognition, and mobile apps.

**Q: Do you offer solar-powered barriers?**
**A:** Yes, we offer Instaboom and Sosec solar-powered barriers, perfect for remote locations or eco-friendly projects.

## Pricing & Installation

**Q: What is the installation cost?**
**A:** Installation cost varies based on barrier type, site conditions, and integration requirements. We provide detailed quotations after site survey.

**Q: Do you provide maintenance?**
**A:** Yes, we offer preventive maintenance contracts, 24/7 emergency support, and annual inspection packages.

**Q: What is the warranty?**
**A:** Warranty varies by product: Standard barriers 2 years, FAAC products 2-3 years, Parklio 1 year. Extended warranties available.

**Q: What is the lead time?**
**A:** Standard barriers: 2-4 weeks. Imported brands (FAAC, Parklio): 4-8 weeks. Custom solutions: 6-10 weeks.

**Q: Can you install in existing structures?**
**A:** Yes, we specialize in retrofit installations in existing buildings and facilities.

## Technical Questions

**Q: How fast do barriers open?**
**A:** Standard boom barriers: 1.5-6 seconds (adjustable). High-speed barriers: < 1 second.

**Q: What happens during power failure?**
**A:** All barriers have battery backup (4-8 hours) and manual override for emergency operation.

**Q: Are barriers suitable for high-security areas?**
**A:** Yes, we offer high-security bollards (PAS 68 rated), full-height turnstiles, and FAAC industrial barriers for maximum security.

**Q: Can I control barriers remotely?**
**A:** Yes, with our management software and mobile apps, you can monitor and control barriers from anywhere.

**Q: Do barriers work in extreme heat?**
**A:** Yes, our barriers are rated for -20°C to +70°C, perfect for Saudi Arabia's climate.

**Q: What safety features do barriers have?**
**A:** Safety sensors, LED warning lights, emergency stop buttons, auto-reverse on obstruction, and manual override.
